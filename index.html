About

I take the approach of building an application from ground up. All the way from building the data model, business logic and UI, And introduce you to advance concepts like VisualForce. You will get access to a free developer environment, which you can use to build and test you application. Every other lecture is followed by an Assignment and a short quiz.

This way you are exposed to different facets of app building.

You can ask questions on the discussion board. And I can virtually support you with any questions / assignments.
